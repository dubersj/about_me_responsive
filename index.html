A Bootstrap 5 Fan

Hello, welcome to my about me webpage made with Bootstrap 5! I am a senior Computer Science student at Kent State University. I plan to graduate this semester, and hope to use what I've learned in this program for personal projects (such as a portfolio) in the interest of finding my niche in one of many vast topics in CS.

Aside from my current college endeavours, I have gained much great experience flying beginning late in high school. One career aspiration of mine is flying for a major airline. The cost of Kent's flight program was a limiting factor, but the CS degree is still preferential experience, and will allow me to find a career to fund the rest of flight training.

Some of my favorite adventures include my first hard IFR flight from Akron to Atlanta, my trip with my dad to Put-In-Bay (3W2), and my 8 hours logged time in a turboprop Piper Cheyenne.
